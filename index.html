What Is Wealth?

Wealth is the accumulation of valuable resources or possessions. For most people, this often includes money, real estate, investments, or businesses. Some also consider health, knowledge, and personal time as forms of modern wealth.

Most people gain wealth through steady work, entrepreneurship, or investing over time. Others may inherit it or acquire it through fame, innovation, or unique opportunities. Wealth can provide freedom and security, but also brings responsibilities in how it’s used and shared.
